Welcome To Our LQPS Family

Little Queen Public School is run by Holy Family sisters under the eminent guidance of Navajyothi province, Thrissur. It is the first CBSE School of the Navajyothi province of holy family congregation. Little Queen Public School is committed to both thought and action. Our eminent faculty and efficient system delivers bold new ideas. We offer our students the opportunity to benefit from a unique blend of world-class education and the state of the art infrastructure. We aim at transforming young minds to dynamic individuals and equip them to face challenges courageously.


Our Mision And Vision

Create a better world of enlightened and integrated citizens and mould ideal families by imparting knowledge of God and value based quality education. Little Queen Public School, Olarikkara is an undertaking of the Holy family Congregation of navajyothy Province. The purpose of this educational Institution is to impart quality education from a very young age and enkindle in the hearts of the children the spirit of courage to be better human persons in life. This institution, right from the time of its inception has made all the effort to facilitate maximum care for children while they are in the school.
