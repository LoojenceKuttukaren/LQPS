ECA Activities

Instrumental Music

This one of the important factors to a child’s development. This gives children a chance to hear the different instruments being used without being distracted with language. When children explore and experiment, regardless of what skills they are working on, they are successful. This sense of accomplishment promotes a child’s desire to extend their learning experiences.

Martial Arts

Allows the kids and their parents to be more active. The children learns to sit with themself and see where their weaknesses are. They also learns what it is to take a hit, whether that hit is a literal blow or a disappointment like failing a test, and get back up.

Yoga

Yoga with children offers many possibilities to exchange wisdom, share good times, and lay the foundation for a lifelong practice that will continue to deepen. All that’s needed is a little flexibility on the adult’s part because, as I quickly found out when I first started teaching the practice to preschoolers, yoga for children is quite different than yoga for adults.
